I am a PhD student in Artificial Intelligence at [Université Paris-Saclay](https://www.universite-paris-saclay.fr/) (Lincoln lab), working on **real-time waste detection by drone** using federated learning and multi-objective optimization. I am also enrolled in the [MVA Master](https://www.master-mva.com/) at ENS Paris-Saclay.

Alongside my research, I teach at [École Centrale d'Electronique](https://www.ece.fr/) (Mathematics, Artificial Intelligence, and MLOps).

## Research Interests

- Federated Learning
- Computer Vision / Object Detection (YOLO, UAV)
- Large Language Models (LLMs) & RAG
- Multi-objective Optimization
- MLOps & Deployment

## Background

Before my PhD, I worked as a **Deep Learning Engineer at Lincoln** (Boulogne-Billancourt), where I built a patented Multi-Modal Retrieval Augmented Generation (RAG) system, and as a **Data Scientist at AtlasAI**, where I contributed to training an LLM for the Moroccan dialect.

I hold a **Double Master's degree in Mathematics, Computer Science, and Data Science (MIDS)** from Université Paris Cité, and a **Bachelor's degree in Mathematics** (with honors) from Université Paris Est Créteil.
